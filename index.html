Fruit Smash

  • High Concept: Attempt to keep the controls in check in order to smash all the fruit.

  • Player Experience Goals: The game should be frustrating and difficult to control.

  • Genre: Casual games.

  • Game play: Hold down your pointer (mouse, finger) in order to create a stream of 'bullets' to smash the fruit.
    System dynamics: The input stream is slow to move and drags behind.
    Core mechanic: You can drag the bullet stream to smash the fruit.

  • Prototype goal: This prototype explores indirect control, something I had not done yet.

  • Features: My game is unique because it has a unique form of control.

  • Setting/Premise: You want to explode all the fruit.

  • Story: There is no story.

  • Target Audience: Casual gamers.

  • Play time: 3 minutes.

  • Strategies: It's non-intuitive, but try rotating the cursor in a circular motion/circles.
